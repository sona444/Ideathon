About Ideathon'22

  • We are pleased to announce that Department of Computer Science and Engineering, FET is organizing 1st National Level Ideathon 1.0. during 18th and 19th February, 2022 under the aegis of, MRIIRS Faridabad, India.
  • We, at Manav Rachna International Institute of Research and Studies, aim to spread the innovation and augmentation in the field of computing, working towards students' holistic development in our institute through teach-back sessions, workshops, events, and online competitions along with special guidance for personality development
  • Ideathon 1.0. envisages a wider reach to the student community and better collaboration with the industry, in order to leverage the potential to find the solutions to most pressing problems of today.
  • The broader areas of Ideathon 1.0. are Banking, Cyber Security, Automation, Environment sustainability, Fintech, Agriculture and Rural Development, Food Technology, Healthcare & Biomedical Devices, Automobile, Robotics & Drones, and Al Systems.
  • MRIIRS Ideathon 1.0. is an initiative towards providing a road map for young minds to identify critical challenges and their solutions, which we are facing in our day-to-day life.
  • It motivates young minds to inculcate their ideas to provide the solutions in terms of developing innovative product or its prototype.
  • Some key points of this event are:
    • We aim to aid the community by employing technology, developing applications or building devices that would help in bringing about a change in their lives.
    • The solutions will be designed primarily focusing on the real-life problems.
    • Any student/research scholar/innovator/inventor from NCR can participate and formulate solutions with the help of technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Security, AR/VR, Audio, Animation, loT, Blockchain and Robotics.
  • Teams must be comprised of 1-4 people.
  • Members of a team can be from the same as well as different years and colleges.
  • All design elements, code, hardware builds, etc. for your project must be created during the event. No development may start before the actual date and time of the event. Any teams that violate this rule will be automatically disqualified.
  • You are permitted to use publicly developed and openly licensed APIâ€™s and SDKs for your project.
  • All prizes are to be shared between all team members.
  • All team members should be present on the day of the event(online).
  • Teams should be made up exclusively of students who are not organizers, volunteers, judges, sponsors, or in any other privileged position at the event.
